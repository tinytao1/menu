Button States for Groceries:

  1. Enabled - - - - - - - - -(default - button is available and active)
  2. Disabled - - - - - - - - (Inactive, unavailable, greyed-out, preventing clicks (using pointer-events: none), button's action is unavailable)
  3. Focus - - - - - - - - - - (or keyboard-focus, Shows keyboard focus, typically a distinct outline, essential for keyboard navigation)
  4. Pressed - - - - - - - - (The user pressed the button )
